Mary Wollstonecraft Shelley - Frankenstein

Welcome to the mini digital edition of chapter 7 of Mary Shelley's Frankenstein. Made by Cor Frederickx as a project for Text as Data course 2024-2025, taught at the University of Antwerp.

First some background on the marriage and literary collaboration between Percy and Mary

This digital edition was based on the digitized manuscript of Mary Wollstonecraft Shelley and Percy Byssche Shelley, as provided by the Shelley-Godwin Archive. The original text can be found in Oxford's Bodleian library. The manuscript with its various alterations was encoded in TEI-compliant XML on the basis of the BDMP Encoding Manual The XML-files are available from the project's GitHub page.
